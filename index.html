As students of psychology,

Our college training is largely theoretical. As if we are trained in everything except: "What do we actually do as a therapist?"

We are sent to the field to fend for ourselves. Often feeling helpless.

And ofcourse, therapists don't earn well.

What Kirana wants to stand for,

A place to support you in your frustration and helplessness.

Help you learn the actual skills through practice and not PPTs.

And move up in the ladder towards employment or starting your own practice.

Our vision,

We want to build a world-class therapist training infrastructure where members (not "students") graduate feeling confident in their skills and in what they can offer to clients.
